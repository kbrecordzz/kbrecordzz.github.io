I have a vision of a video game "console" on the web. A webpage with maybe 10 really good games and none of the things you don't want (ads, etc). Something with a level of ambition between games where you click on bubbles (typical mobile games) and games where you practice for 7 years to beat a dragon (Elden Ring). Something like Super Mario, Pokemon and Zelda, but completely different, and without having to buy a console for $300.
